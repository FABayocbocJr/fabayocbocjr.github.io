Franis A. Bayocboc, Jr. is a research assistant at The University of Queensland, 
where he studies nonequilibrium dynamics of quantum many-body systems, 
in particular one-dimensional quasicondensates. He received a PhD in 
Physics from The University of Queensland in 2021, where he was advised 
by Prof. Karen Kheruntsyan. Previously, he received a MSc in Physics 
from the University of the Philippines, Diliman in 2015, and a BSc in 
Physics from the Ateneo de Manila University in 2013.
