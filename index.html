Pax et bonum!

I am a Research Assistant in the School of Mathematics and Physics at the University of Queensland, where I study the nonequilibrium dynamics of quantum many-body systems, in particular one-dimensional quasicondensates.

I received my PhD in Physics from the University of Queensland in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Karén V. Kheruntsyan. Before coming to Australia, I was a faculty member in the Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Physics at the University of the Philippines, Los Baños from 2015 to 2017. Previously, I received my MSc in Physics from the University of the Philippines, Diliman in 2015, and a BSc in Physics from the Ateneo de Manila University in 2013.

Research

My primary research interests are in the area of theoretical quantum many-body physics. In particular, I am interested in the equilibrium and nonequilibrium properties of low-dimensional quantum systems such as degenerate quantum gases, Bose-Einstein condensates, and spin systems.
